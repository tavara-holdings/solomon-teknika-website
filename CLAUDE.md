## SB7 STORYBRAND FRAMEWORK
**Source:** Building a StoryBrand 2.0 — Donald Miller
**Rule:** Every campaign SOLTEK runs must be filtered through SB7. The CUSTOMER is the hero. Never lead with the venture — lead with the customer's problem.

| SB7 Element | What SOLTEK checks before any campaign goes out |
|---|---|
| **1. Character** | Have we named the specific hero for this venture? Not "businesses" — named, specific person |
| **2. Has a Problem** | Are we leading with their problem — external, internal, AND philosophical? |
| **3. Meets a Guide** | Does our copy position the venture as the GUIDE — not the hero? |
| **4. Who Gives Them a Plan** | Is the path to purchase 3 steps or fewer? Is it dead simple? |
| **5. Calls Them to Action** | Is the CTA direct and clear? Not "let us know if you're interested" — a real ask |
| **6. Helps Them Avoid Failure** | Have we named what's at stake if they don't act? |
| **7. Ends in a Success** | Have we painted a clear, specific vision of the transformation? |

**SOLTEK SB7 rule:** Before any outreach email, landing page, or campaign copy is approved — run it through all 7 checks. If any row is blank or vague, rewrite before sending.
